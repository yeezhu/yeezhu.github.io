I am a Phd student in the PRI-SDL at UCAS, advised by Prof. Jianbin Jiao and Prof. Qixiang Ye. I got the B.Eng. in Software Engineering at SYSU. My research is in computer vision and machine learning. I work on visual recognition (object recognition, image segmentation, scene classification) and weakly supervised learning.
